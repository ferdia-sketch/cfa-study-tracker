When Things Go Wrong — Rules to Follow

🚩 3+ Days Behind Schedule

Add 3–4 extra hours on the weekend. If still behind after 1 week: reduce EOCQ depth for the current topic and move on. Breadth beats depth.

🚩 Below 30% on Topic EOCQs

Flag the topic for the review period. Do NOT re-study it now. Keep moving forward. You'll come back to it in October with fresh eyes.

🚩 Missed 2+ Study Days in a Row

Take 1 full day completely off. Drop next week's target to 12 hours. Reassess your schedule. Burnout is a bigger risk than falling behind.

🚩 Feeling Like Quitting

Look at your hours logged on this tracker. You ARE doing the work. Scoring 30% on first-pass EOCQs is completely normal. Talk to someone about how you're feeling.

🚩 Panicking Entering Review Period

This is expected and normal. The 30-day review period exists precisely for this reason. Trust the process. Focus on Ethics + high-weight topics first.

🚩 QM Sprint Feels Too Fast

LM6 (Simulation Methods) and LM11 (Big Data Techniques) are lighter modules — you'll catch your breath there. Push through the harder ones.
